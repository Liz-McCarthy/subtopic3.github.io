Benefits of Technology in Sports

Technology is an imperative part of sports. It is used for injury prevention, and broadcasting so that everybody can watch and enjoy while players are kept healthy.



    Site 1; Author: Yellowbrick

    		 "The Impact of Technology in Sports Performance"

    Abstract:

    In this article, there are many aspects of technology in sports that show how it changes the game. It mentions how the technology is helpful for injury prevention, and performance of athletes. Technology is also useful for connecting teams and players to fans on social media which helps to market sports. Video replays are useful for referees making calls on the field, and keeping the game fair as well as sports broadcasting so that fans from anywhere can tune in and watch their favorite athletes play. Overall, the benefits are growing from technology as it is researched, and it helps each sport be where it is today.


    Site 2; Authors: Thalia Oosthuizen, Deirdre Hunter

    		 "Sports technology that's Revolutionizing Sports in 2024"

    Abstract:

    In this article, the authors touch on different technology used in sports and how each one can impact separate parts of the game. It explains how things like virtual reality can help athletes perfect their skills by using game like visuals. It also talks about injury prevention, and technology in sports rehab that allows for quicker recovery. Fan engagement is noted as well, and "Goal Line Technology" that helps the referees make calls for goals in sports like soccer. General gadgets like wearable technology is listed in this article, and the beauty of online scheduling for games and events is mentioned as an important technology as well.
